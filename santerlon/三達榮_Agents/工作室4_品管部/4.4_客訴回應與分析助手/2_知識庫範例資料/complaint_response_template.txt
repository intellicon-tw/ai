# 客訴回應範本

## 客戶回應範本（英文，正式）

```
Subject: Quality Issue Response - PO [PO No.]

Dear [Customer],

We sincerely apologize for the quality issue reported on PO [PO No.].

Investigation Result:
Root Cause: [Manufacturing / Material / Packaging / Design / Logistics]
Details: [Detailed cause description]

Corrective Action:
- [Action 1, e.g., Re-inspect remaining stock]
- [Action 2, e.g., Replace defective items]

Preventive Measure:
- [Prevention 1, e.g., Strengthened QC SOP]
- [Prevention 2, e.g., Material supplier review]

Compensation:
[Replacement / Credit / Discount on next order]

We take full responsibility and appreciate your patience.
We are committed to delivering the quality you expect from Sandarong.

Best regards,
[Name]
QC Department
```
