# Few-shot 文案範例(內嵌至提示詞用)

> 本文件提供 3 個「轉換率高 / 客戶反應好」的真實風格範例,涵蓋 EDM、LinkedIn 貼文、展會邀約。
> **使用方式**:把這些範例**內嵌到 Agent 提示詞的「執行流程」末尾**,作為 Few-shot 教學素材,讓 AI 模仿這些範例的語氣、結構、結尾方式。
> 這就是 Few-shot Learning 的精神 —— 「給 AI 看『好的答案』長什麼樣子」。

================================================================

範例 2:LinkedIn 貼文(技術分享,目標客群 D — 海外客戶)
================================================================

**情境**:LinkedIn 公司頁面發布技術洞察文,吸引海外 Tier 2 / 整合商
**主題**:溫度感測器在低溫應用的設計挑戰
**字數**:240 字
**語氣**:專業國際化、教育性質

---

🌡️ **Why Most Temperature Sensors Fail Below -30°C — And How We Engineered Around It**

In the past 12 months, we've worked with three OEM teams designing for cold-chain logistics, EV battery management, and aerospace test rigs. They share one common challenge:

📉 **Temperature drift at the low end (-40°C to -20°C)** — even when datasheets claim ±1°C accuracy, real-world deviations often exceed ±2.5°C.

The root cause? Calibration equipment itself drifts at extreme temperatures. Most factories validate sensors at room temperature and trust the linearity curve.

🔬 **Our approach with PROD-E-001:**
1. **Three-point validation** — every batch validated at -40°C, 25°C, and 125°C
2. **Monthly calibration equipment recertification** — not annually
3. **Per-batch deviation report** delivered with shipment

The result: customers can confidently design for cold-chain applications without adding redundant compensation circuits.

Looking to validate this for your next platform? Drop us a message — we'd be glad to share the full validation methodology.

教學重點:Few-shot 為什麼有效?
================================================================

對比兩種提示詞:

❌ **沒有 Few-shot 的提示詞**:
"請為 PROD-A-001 寫一封 EDM,目標客群是 OEM 大廠。"
→ AI 可能寫得太冷、太行銷化、太多形容詞、結尾不明確。

✅ **有 Few-shot 的提示詞**:
"請為 PROD-A-001 寫一封 EDM,目標客群是 OEM 大廠。
請參考以下範例的結構、語氣、CTA 寫法:
[範例 1 的完整內容]"
→ AI 會模仿範例的「先給三個具體改進、用數據支持、CTA 提議 30 分鐘技術會議、PS 提供文件」這套結構。

**重點**:Few-shot 不是給「題目」,而是給「答案的模樣」。
